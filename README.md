### <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MartinHeinz/MartinHeinz/master/wave.gif" width="30px"> About Me

> “Data pipelines should be like good coffee—strong, reliable, and capable of keeping you up at night.” ☕

I'm Lokmane, a final-year Data Engineering and Data Science student at ENSA Al Hoceima, passionate about transforming complex data into strategic insights. I'm on a mission to turn siloed city parking into live, actionable intelligence, currently interning at **STM (Tanger-Tétouan)** where I'm building the backbone for a real-time parking network using **Kafka & Debezium**.

My core interest lies in building robust data pipelines, big data infrastructures, and next-generation applications powered by generative AI. Outside of work and studies, I’m either debugging Airflow DAGs or perfecting my homemade mint ATAY (Moroccan tea). 🍵

- 🔭 I’m currently building a **Real-Time Parking Grid** with CDC pipelines (Kafka + Debezium) feeding a central PostgreSQL.
- 🌱 I’m deepening my expertise in **Data Engineering industry tools** through hands-on projects and exploring the **Spring Framework**.
- 👯 I’m eager to collaborate on **NLP projects for the Arabic language**.
- 💬 Ask me about **Kafka, Debezium, Airflow, FastAPI, or anything Data Engineering!**
- 🏆 **Upwork Rising Talent** badge for top-rated data engineering gigs.
- 🎤 Speaker at local **DevOps meetups** on Kafka scalability.
